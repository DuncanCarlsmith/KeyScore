Track FX panel

fx button
Click fx at the right of each track row to reveal tone, room, and pan controls.
tone
Low-pass filter. Left = dark/muffled (200 Hz). Right = fully open (20 kHz). Default: fully right.
room
Reverb wet amount. 0% = dry. 5-15% = subtle warmth. High values = concert hall effect.
pan
Stereo placement. L100 = hard left. Center = default. R100 = hard right.
volume
Range is 0-200%. Values above 100% add up to +6 dB -- use to compensate for perceived level loss when tone is darkened or room is increased.

Recording

1. Click score
Move gold cursor to set recording start.
2. Click rec
Countdown plays, then recording begins.
3. Play, click stop
Score freezes. Piece extends if needed.
